MetaKoopman

A Koopman-Based Framework for Meta-Learning Dynamical Systems

Mahmoud Selim, [Add co-authors if applicable]

Abstract

MetaKoopman introduces a data-efficient meta-learning framework for discovering Koopman operators that generalize across dynamical systems. By combining Koopman theory with meta-learning principles, the method enables rapid adaptation to new systems with minimal training data, achieving state-of-the-art performance on benchmark dynamical prediction tasks.

Highlights

  • Unified Koopman and meta-learning formulation for continuous-time systems.
  • Enables generalization across families of dynamical systems.
  • Compatible with neural operator and autoencoder architectures.
  • Open-source implementation for reproducible research.
